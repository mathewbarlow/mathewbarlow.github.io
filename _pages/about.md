I am a Professor of Climate Science at the University of Massachusetts Lowell. I am a physical climate scientist interested in extreme events, including both hot and cold events as well as wet and dry events. I study the causes of these events and how they are changing in a warming climate. I am also interested in ways to engage with the public and decision-makers in the communication of scientific results.

The pages here provide further information on my professional work in terms of publications, research, science communication, data visualization, legal work, and CV.
