My research interests focus on the influence of climate on local conditions of importance to society. I am interested in the influence of climate both in terms of natural variability and climate change and I am interested in a range of local conditions, including both temperature and precipitation-related extremes, like heatwaves, cold snaps, droughts, and heavy rain. With respect to understanding these extremes, I am interested in the physical processes that cause them, how well we can predict them, and how they are changing in a warming climate. Some of my long-standing research areas include:  
  
  * US Precipitation extremes, especially over the Northeast  
  * US Temperature extremes, especially over the Northeast  
  * Drought in the Middle East and Southwest Asia  
  * The role of the stratosphere in cold air outbreaks
  
I am also broadly interested in atmospheric wave dynamics, the links between the atmosphere and the ocean, and the water cycle, aspects of which are common themes throughout my work.
